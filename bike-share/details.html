Bike Share

Build support for bike share by engaging the community in suggesting locations, or get feedback on a shortlist of possible sites. Use on-the-ground local wisdom to pick the best spots, and demonstrate support from a diverse range of participants.

  • Collect suggested locations for bike share.
  • Gather feedback about existing locations.
  • Engage people with address lookup for easy location finding on desktop or mobile.
  • Follow up with confirmation emails to participants.
  • Track activity with daily email reports.
  • Get the complete picture with the OpenPlans Dashboard.
