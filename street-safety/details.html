Street Safety

Too many people are injured while walking and biking. Gather information about street safety issues so that street design changes can be informed by knowledge from many participants.

  • Easily collect & manage safety concerns.
  • Differentiate categories with beautiful icons.
  • Engage people with address lookup for easy location finding on desktop or mobile.
  • Follow up with confirmation emails to participants.
  • Track activity with daily email reports.
  • Get the complete picture with the OpenPlans Dashboard.
